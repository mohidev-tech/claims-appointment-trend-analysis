How to read this dashboard.
  • Cancellation rate = cancelled appointments ÷ total appointments; completion rate = completed ÷ total.
  • Claim approval rate = (Approved + Paid) claims ÷ total claims.
  • Aging buckets group claims by days since submission: 0-30, 31-60, 61-90 and 90+ days; Denied and In-Review claims skew toward the older buckets.
  • Avg processing days = claim-count-weighted mean of processing time; the rising trend highlights a growing processing bottleneck.
  • KPI deltas compare the latest month in range against the prior month (month-over-month).
  • All figures are synthetic and reproducible (seed = 42). Dashboard totals reconcile exactly to the SQL queries in /sql.
